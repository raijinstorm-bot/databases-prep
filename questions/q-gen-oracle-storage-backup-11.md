Which statements about Oracle control files are correct?

- a) They are small binary files (typically under 64MB)
- b) They are required to mount or open a database
- c) They are of primary interest to end users rather than DBAs
- d) A database should have a minimum of two, with three preferred on different disks

### Explanation

Control files are small binary files (a), describe the database structure, and are required to mount/open the database (b). They should be multiplexed, with at least two and preferably three on different disks (d). They are of interest to DBAs only, so (c) is false.
