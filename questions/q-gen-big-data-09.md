What is the central idea of the "long tail" model?

- a) The overall sales volume of a large group of unpopular products may exceed the sales of the few most successful products
- b) Only the few best-selling products ever generate any profit
- c) Products should be removed if they do not sell in high quantities
- d) Sales follow a uniform distribution across all products

### Explanation

The long tail model holds that the combined sales of many unpopular products can exceed those of the limited number of best-sellers (a) — a cornerstone of Amazon's success. The other statements contradict this idea.
