Which of the following is a typical category of vector layer listed in the presentation?

- a) Bitmap
- b) Orthophoto
- c) Polyline (linia łamana)
- d) Tile

### Explanation

The typical vector layer categories listed are Polyline, Point and Polygon. Bitmap, orthophoto and tile are associated with raster layers, not vector layer categories.
