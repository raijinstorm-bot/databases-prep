Which of the following are examples of raster layers?

- a) Scanned paper-based maps
- b) Orthophotos (aerial photos) and satellite images
- c) A layer of address points
- d) A polyline layer of rivers

### Explanation

Raster layers include scanned paper maps, orthophotos and satellite images, stored as bitmaps tied to geocoordinates. Address points and rivers (polylines) are vector-layer objects, not raster.
