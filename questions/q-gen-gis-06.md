What does GIS stand for?

- a) Geodatabase Indexing System
- b) Geographical Information System
- c) Global Imaging Service
- d) Geometric Integration Standard

### Explanation

GIS stands for Geographical Information System (System Informacji przestrzennej): a collection of hardware, software and geographic data for capturing, managing, analyzing and displaying geographically referenced information. The other expansions are invented.
