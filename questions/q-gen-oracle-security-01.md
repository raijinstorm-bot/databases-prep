According to the security principles in the presentation, applying the principle of least privilege includes which of the following?

- a) Locking user accounts that are not used
- b) Revoking privileges that are not needed
- c) Granting users only the privileges they actually require
- d) Granting all privileges to PUBLIC for convenience

### Explanation

Least privilege means giving only the privileges that are truly needed (c), locking unused accounts (a) and revoking unneeded privileges (b). Granting privileges broadly to PUBLIC is exactly the unsafe practice the principle warns against, so (d) is wrong.
