Which statements about Business Intelligence (BI) systems are correct?

- a) Having a data warehouse automatically means having a BI system
- b) BI tools let users transform data into useful information
- c) BI systems include reporting, querying, data analysis and data visualization
- d) BI systems can use data from OLAP platforms such as data cubes or directly from data warehouses
- e) BI is used exclusively by software developers, never by business staff

### Explanation

BI tools turn data into useful information (b), provide reporting/querying/analysis/visualization (c), and can draw from cubes or directly from warehouses (d). Having a warehouse does not by itself constitute a BI system (a), and BI is primarily used by business administrative staff (e).
