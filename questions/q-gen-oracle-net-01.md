What does the acronym TNS, the foundation of Oracle Net Services, stand for?

- a) Transport Network Service
- b) Transparent Network Substrate
- c) Transactional Naming System
- d) TCP Network Stack

### Explanation

TNS stands for Transparent Network Substrate, and together with Oracle Net Services it makes connections independent of the operating system and communication protocols. The other expansions are invented and do not appear in the presentation.
