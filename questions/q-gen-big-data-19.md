Which of the following is one of the five types of Hadoop data, paired with a correct objective?

- a) Relational ledger data — to enforce referential integrity
- b) Clickstream data — to understand how people move on a portal and perform customer segmentation
- c) Spreadsheet data — to replace OLAP cubes
- d) Backup data — to guarantee ACID transactions

### Explanation

Clickstream data is one of the five Hadoop data types, used to understand user navigation paths and perform granular customer segmentation (b). The other pairings are invented; the genuine categories are social media, IT/server logs, clickstream, sensor and geolocation data.
