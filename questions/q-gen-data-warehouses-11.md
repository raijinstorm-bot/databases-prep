A "measure" in a data warehouse is typically:

- a) a textual label used to filter facts
- b) a numerical attribute of a fact used to gauge business performance
- c) a foreign key linking to a dimension table
- d) the date of a recorded event

### Explanation

A measure is typically a numerical attribute of a fact (e.g. sales in USD, number of complaints, labour hours) used as a measure of business performance. Labels and foreign keys belong to dimensions, and a date is a dimension attribute.
