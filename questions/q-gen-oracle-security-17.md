Which audit view records logon attempts (successful and/or unsuccessful, depending on settings)?

- a) DBA_AUDIT_OBJECT
- b) DBA_AUDIT_SESSION
- c) DBA_AUDIT_STATEMENT
- d) DBA_FGA_AUDIT_TRAIL

### Explanation

DBA_AUDIT_SESSION records logon attempts. DBA_AUDIT_OBJECT records actions on database objects, DBA_AUDIT_STATEMENT records SQL statements, and DBA_FGA_AUDIT_TRAIL holds fine-grained auditing records.
