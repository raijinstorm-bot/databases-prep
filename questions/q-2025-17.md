The company needs a system that should easily answer queries such as finding all water supply pipes located at least partly in the city area defined by a polygon. This means that the company needs:

- a) GIS system
- b) NoSQL system
- c) RDBMS system
- d) Apache HBase

### Explanation

Finding features that lie within / intersect a polygon is a spatial query over geographic data, which is exactly what a GIS provides (a). Plain NoSQL, RDBMS or HBase do not natively offer spatial layers and operators.
