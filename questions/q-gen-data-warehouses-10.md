Which of the following is the best example of a dimension in dimensional modelling?

- a) Sales in USD
- b) Number of customer complaints
- c) Number of labour hours
- d) Time decomposed into year/quarter/month/week/day

### Explanation

A dimension is an attribute or set of hierarchical attributes used to group facts — time (year/quarter/month/...) is a classic example (d). The other options (sales in USD, number of complaints, labour hours) are numerical measures.
