According to the presentation, which of the following can DDL be used to manage?

- a) Tables
- b) Constraints
- c) Indexes
- d) Individual record values

### Explanation

DDL manages data types, structures and constraints — including tables, constraints, indexes, databases/tablespaces and permissions. Individual record values are manipulated by DML (INSERT/UPDATE/DELETE), not DDL.
