In the ETL acronym, the three steps stand for:

- a) Extract, Transfer, Link
- b) Extract, Transform, Load
- c) Evaluate, Transform, Load
- d) Extract, Translate, List

### Explanation

ETL stands for Extract, Transform, Load — data is extracted from sources, transformed, and loaded into the warehouse. ELT reorders the last two (Extract, Load, Transform), but the letters themselves are Extract/Transform/Load.
