Which naming method is described as the most frequently used and relies on the tnsnames.ora file?

- a) Directory naming
- b) External naming
- c) Local naming
- d) Easy Connect naming

### Explanation

Local naming uses tnsnames.ora to map service aliases to connect descriptors and is described as the most frequently used naming method. Directory naming uses LDAP, external naming uses non-Oracle services such as NIS, and Easy Connect needs no file.
